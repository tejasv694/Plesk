Step 2. Create your custom fields

Creating 'Custom Fields' allows you to collect any data that you'd like from your subscribers. This includes 'Name', 'Sex', 'Age' or any other information.

When a user subscribes to your mailing list they will be asked to enter the requested information. You can then send emails to only certain subscribers, filtering them based on custom field values. For example, you can send different email campaigns to 'male' and 'female' subscribers.
