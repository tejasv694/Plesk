Getting Started

Step 6. Sending your email campaign

Once your email has been created it's time to send it to your subscribers. If you don't have any subscribers you can skip this step until you've built up your mailing list.

You can send your email campaign immediately or if you have CRON turned on (see documentation or ask your administrator) you can set email campaigns to be sent at a future date.

Send your email campaign

Congratulations, you've now sent your first email campaign.
